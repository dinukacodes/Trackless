Frequently Asked Questions

What is NO-CRUMBS?

NO-CRUMBS is a powerful tool designed to protect your online privacy. It blocks trackers, clears cookies, and ensures a safer browsing experience with minimal effort.

What are trackers?

Trackers are invisible scripts used by websites to monitor your online activity. They collect data about your browsing habits, which is often used for targeted ads. Trackless prevents these trackers from following you.

Why should I block trackers?

Blocking trackers helps protect your privacy by stopping companies from collecting your browsing data. It also speeds up website loading times and reduces unnecessary data usage.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files websites store on your device to remember preferences, logins, and other details. While some are useful, others track your activity across different sites. Trackless allows you to clear them easily.

Why should I clear cookies?

Clearing cookies helps remove stored tracking data, frees up space, and can fix certain website issues. It enhances your privacy by reducing online fingerprinting.

What is Whitelist Mode?

Whitelist Mode lets you allow specific trusted websites to bypass tracking protection. This is useful for sites that require cookies or tracking to function properly.

How do I enable Whitelist Mode?

In the NO-CRUMBS popup, toggle on Whitelist Mode and add websites you want to exclude from tracking protection. This gives you control over which sites can store cookies and trackers.

Is NO-CRUMBS always active?

No, you can toggle NO-CRUMBS on or off from the popup. When active, it blocks trackers, enforces secure browsing, and helps maintain your privacy.

How do I know if NO-CRUMBS is working?

The popup displays real-time stats on the number of blocked trackers and cleared cookies, confirming that NO-CRUMBS is actively protecting you.

Will NO-CRUMBS slow down my browser?

No, NO-CRUMBS is optimized to run efficiently. In fact, blocking trackers can speed up website loading times by preventing unnecessary scripts from running.

Does NO-CRUMBS collect my data?

No, NO-CRUMBS is designed to enhance your privacy. It does not collect, store, or share your browsing data.
